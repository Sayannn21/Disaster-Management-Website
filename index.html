Recent Disasters -

These are the recent disasters in India. Trained search and rescue teams need to move around to find such people, and then help or carry them out to safety. If such persons are injured they also require immediate medical attention. and the local municipality are the crucial emergency personnel/agencies in the immediate aftermath of disasters.

2022 Amarnath floods.

At least 13 people, mainly Amarnath pilgrims, have died and dozens went missing after flash floods caused landslips near the Baltal base camp in central Kashmir’s Ganderbal area on Friday.

2022 Balrampur floods.

In October 2022, heavy rainfall caused floods in Balrampur, in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. Over 1300 villages were affected by this flood.

A fire at Bharuch hospital

On 1 May 2021, a fire in a hospital in Bharuch, Gujarat, India, killed at least 16 COVID-19 patients and 2 nurses. The Chief Minister of Gujarat, Vijay Rupani announced ex-gratia aid of ₹4 lakh (US$5,000) for kin of victims.

An earthquake in Assam

Another major earthquake that struck Assam was on August 6, 1988. Over 100 people lost their lives in this earthquake. In 2023, Assam experienced two earthquakes of magnitude 3.6 and 2.8 on March 18.
